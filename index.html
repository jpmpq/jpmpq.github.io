"Dual Bridge" gamemode

This is a sub-gamemode I created based on the popular "The Bridge" maps, featured on a multitude of community servers. These maps can hold a lot more players than the originals (being mostly 1v1/2v2/5v5), as these maps are suitable for 12v12/26v26 all the way up to 32v32, and some of them even combine Bridge with other popular Minecraft gamemodes like Destroy the Monument (also a first).
