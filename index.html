Minecraft PvP level design

A complete repository of my maps and gamemodes for the PvP Game Manager plugin can be found here

"Double Bridge" gamemode

This is a sub-gamemode I created and maintain based on the popular "The Bridge" maps featured on a multitude of Minecraft servers. These maps can hold a lot more players than the original gamemode can (those being mostly 1v1/2v2/5v5), as these maps are suitable for 12v12/26v26 all the way up to 32v32, and some of them even combine Bridge with other popular Minecraft gamemodes like Destroy the Monument or King of the Hill.
